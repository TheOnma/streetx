Our Story

StreetX is a streetwear brand that was founded in 2023 by Precious Onma Adekwu. Growing up in Nigeria, Onma was always drawn to the energy of the streets. She loved the way the vibrant colors, textures, and patterns of traditional Nigerian clothing told stories and conveyed a sense of pride and identity. As she got older, she began to develop an appreciation for streetwear - the way people used clothing to express themselves and their individuality.

And so, StreetX was born. The brand was founded with the purpose of inspiring and empowering the next generation of streetwear enthusiasts to be bold, authentic, and unapologetic. The brand is based in Abuja, Nigeria.
